About

The Oman Medical Specialty Board Dermatology Residency Training Program is structured over 4 years. The first year consists of rotations in General Dermatology, Plastic Surgery, Clinical Genetics, Infectious Disease, Emergency Medicine and others. The next three years include extensive training in general dermatology and related subspecialties. Training is conducted in the hospitals and polyclinics accredited by OMSB.
